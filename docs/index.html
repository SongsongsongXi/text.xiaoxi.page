About the Journal

Science & Humanities Review is a peer-reviewed, international Open Access journal dedicated to fostering dialogue between the sciences and the humanities. We publish original research, reviews, and perspectives that bridge these two vital fields of human knowledge.

Published in the USA by Scientia Profunda Academic Press, we strive for academic excellence and broad accessibility.
